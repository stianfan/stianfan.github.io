I'm a full-stack enjoyer with roots in the 90s tech scene, having built and fixed everything from beige box PCs to modern cloud architectures. These days, I channel my creative energy into web development, some Godot and electronic music production.

My music journey started behind a drum kit in punk bands, evolved through running a recording studio, and landed me in the world of electronic music production.

That same DIY ethic now drives my approach to web development – I believe in lean, efficient solutions that don't sacrifice modern functionality. You won't find bloated JavaScript or unnecessary cookie banners here; just clean, fast, and accessible designs that get the job done.
